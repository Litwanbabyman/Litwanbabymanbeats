Know Before You Buy

Licensing, plainly

General terms below — the exact rights for each purchase are spelled out in the license agreement at checkout.

Lease

MP3 License

The cheapest way in. Good for demos, mixtapes, and non-profit releases. You get an MP3 and a standard lease — the beat stays available to other artists too.

Trackout

WAV + Stems

Full WAV plus separated stems, so a mix engineer can shape the drums, bass, and melody independently. Best once a song is heading toward a real release.

Exclusive

Full Ownership

The beat is pulled from the store and the rights transfer to you. One buyer, full ownership, no one else can license it after.
